# Ship Happens (working title)

A modern reimagining of the classic game "Ports of Call" (1986, Rolf-Dieter Klein & Martin Ulrich).

## About the game

Ports of Call is a shipping simulation game where the player runs a shipping company. The original was available on Amiga and DOS.

### Core mechanics (from the original)

- **World map/globe**: Navigate between ports around the world
- **Office**: Manage the company
- **Ship Broker**: Buy and sell ships
- **Port operations**: Upon arriving at a port, the player can:
  - REPAIR – repair the ship
  - REFUEL – refuel
  - CHARTER – accept freight contracts
  - LAY UP – put the ship in lay-up
  - LOAD – load cargo
- **Port maneuvering**: Steer the ship into the harbor (minigame with time limit)
- **Economy**: Bank account, freight costs, fuel, maintenance
- **Ship status**: Condition (%), fuel level, cargo capacity (tdw)
- **Simulation time**: Weeks and years ticking forward

### Ports (examples from the original)

Rio de Janeiro, London, Rotterdam, New York, Karachi, etc.
